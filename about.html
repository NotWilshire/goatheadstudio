ABOUT

HEY, WHAT'S UP WORLD

I'm Chip the guy behind Goat Head Studio. I help people tell digital stories that actually connect.

I started my professional career at Mississippi Public Broadcasting under the producer who now runs All Things Considered at NPR. From there, I was a multimedia journalist for a statewide network, earning a national award with our news team in 2014.

Since, I've produced content and created marketing strategies for small newsrooms, billion-dollar companies, and state agencies. I received a People's Choice Podcast nomination in 2024 for the Mississippi Outdoors Podcast.

If your project sounds interesting, let's talk. I take on work when it feels like the right fit.
